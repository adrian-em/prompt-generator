Instructions

This tool is very basic and easy to use, but just to clarify:

You have some predefined variables to use, or you can enter a custom text. By custom text I mean everything, @, :, [], {}, whatever you want to include.

For example, if you want to show your username, working directory, or anything variable in your system you should use the predefined items.

When you add an element, the form will be emptied to enter the next one. Also, you can watch a more or less accurate preview of your future prompt and the code you will need to copy to your $PS1 when finished.
